Claims Procedure for Airport Users

Purpose

To regulate the claiming and restitution process in the case of harm inflicted as designated by the Aviation Act and other related laws

Filing a claim: process and location

  • How to file a claim : Fill out the compensation request form for Airport-users 다운로드
  • Submission location
    • Submit in person : Incheon International Airport Information Desk(Floor 1, Floor 3)
    • Submit by e-mail : voc@airport.kr

Processing customer claim and assigned departments

    Within 14 days of submission
  • Apply for compensation(Claim submission)
  • Accept submission(Passenger Service Team)
  • Accept submission(Passenger Service Team)
  • Assign to proper department with jurisdiction(CS Management Team)
  • Provide contact information for relevant airline, other agency
  • Verify damages claimed (Assigned department)
  • Receive legal advice(Legal Department)
  • When necessary
  • Verify facts(Assigned department)
  • Oversee outcome effect(CS Management Team)
  • Verify facts(Assigned department)
  • Communicate outcome(Assigned department)
  • Claimant accepts outcome
  • Claimant rejects outcome
  • Proceed with compensation
  • Transfer to the Korea Consumer Agency

At the request of the claimant, the case can be transferred even within the 14-day period to the Korea Consumer Agency.

Receiving the claim outcome

Within 14 days after the submission, the claimant will receive the claim outcome through the method specified at the time of submission (website, e-mail, SMS, phone call, fax).

Except, the processing period can take up to 60 days in the case of special claims including claims requiring translation and claims with a unique context.

Repealing the claim outcome

When the claimant repeals the outcome (in person, through an official call, online), the assigned department and the claim registration will forward the claim submission to the Korea Consumer Agency.
