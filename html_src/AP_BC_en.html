Arrival Procedure
01
Arriving at Incheon Airport

Passengers using Gates 1-50 enter through Passenger Terminal 1.

Passengers using Gates 101-132 use the shuttle from the Concourse to proceed to Passenger Terminal 1.

Passengers using Gates 230-270 enter through Passenger Terminal 2.

02
Immigration Process
Immigration Screening
Automated screening

Korean nationals and registered foreigners do not need to fill out the entry form. Only unregistered foreigners must fill out the entry form

03
Baggage Claim
Baggage Claim
Lost or Damaged Baggage

After completing the immigration process, check the baggage claim number. Take the escalators to Floor 1 and pick up your baggage.

04
Warning/Reporting
