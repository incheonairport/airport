Quarantine Inspection

QuarantineDeclaration

All passengers (nationals and foreigners) entering from another country are subject to quarantine inspection. In the case of entry with animals or related products, you must visit the Animal and Plant Quarantine Inspection Agency Headquarters to submit the quarantine inspection approval form from the origin country and be inspected.

Contagious disease requiring quarantine

검역대상 전염병 안내표
Type Contagious Diseases
Fish 8 Epizootic haematopoietic necrosis, spring viraemia of carp, viral haemorrhagic septicaemia, infectious salmon anaemia, red sea bream iridoviral disease, koi herpres virus disease, epizootic ulcerative syndrome, gyrodactylus salaris
Shellfish 5 Bonamia infection (bonamia ostrea, bonamia exitiosa), marteilia refringens infection, perkinsus marinus infection, xenohaliotis californiensis infection, abalone herpes virus infection
Crustacean 6 Crayfish plague, infection hypodermal and haematopoietie necrosis, yellow head disease, white spot disease, taura syndrome, infection myonerosis, white tail disease

The list contains diseases that apply to each type of fishery product.

Quarantine inspection process

검역방법 안내표
Type Quarantine Inspection Procedure Length of Inspection Details
Import Document inspection 2 days Check the validity of quarantine inspection request form and attached documents
Clinic inspection 3 days Check the quarantine subject’s form and behavior in conjunction with external opinion and anatomic study
Comprehensive inspection 15 days Incorporate biological analysis including histopathological, molecular biological, and serological studies
