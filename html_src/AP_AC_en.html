출국 전 준비

Finish all business before entering the departure area. Currency exchange, Withdrawal, Roaming, Insurance & Misc.

05

you have legally left the country.

Regular security check
Fast track
Check Prohibited items Prohibited items FAQ
06
Immigration Process

You cannot return to the public
accessible area, and you cannot use
the ATMs in the duty free zone.

In-person screening
Automated screening
07
Proceed to Boarding Gate

Proceed after verifying the gate number and location on the boarding pass.

Passengers using Gates 1-50 may proceed to Passenger Terminal 1.

Passengers using Gates 101-132 should take the shuttle train from Passenger Terminal 1 to Concourse.

Once you leave the terminal, you cannot return.

Passengers using Gates 230-270 board at Passenger Terminal 2.

08
Passenger Boarding

Proceed to gate or concourse 30-40 minutes before departure time and board the flight.
