Quarantine Inspection

QuarantineDeclaration

All passengers (nationals and foreigners) entering from another country are subject to quarantine inspection. In the case of entry with animals or related products, you must visit the Animal and Plant Quarantine Inspection Agency Headquarters to submit the quarantine inspection approval form from the origin country and be inspected.

검역 관련 절차는 탑승수속을 받기 전에 하셔야 신속하게 탑승하실 수 있습니다.

Plant inspection

  • Passengers with live plants, fruit, vegetable, agricultural product, forest product, flower, seedling, oriental medicine, etc. must mark the appropriate box on the Customs possession declaration form and be inspected by a quarantine officer.
  • You must submit the quarantine inspection approval form from the origin country for sapling, seedling, root, and any vegetation for replanting.
  • All types of vegetation from abroad are the key route for alien parasite transfer, so all plants carried on flight must be reported and are subject to quarantine inspection

You will be fined for illegally carrying in plants without inspection.
