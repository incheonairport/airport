Prohibition on Unauthorized Collection of Electronic Mail Addresses

This website rejects the unauthorized collection of the E-mail addresses displayed within through the use of E-mail extractors or other devices, and violations of this rejection will receive criminal punishment in accordance with the Act on Promotion of Utilization of Information and Communications Network.

[Posted June 1st, 2004]

Act on Information and Communications Network Article 50-2 (Prohibition on Unauthorized Collection of Electronic Mail Addresses)

  • 1. No one may collect electronic mail addresses from an internet homepage without prior consent of the operator or manager of the internet homepage automatically by a program for collecting electronic mail addresses or any other technical device.
  • 2. No one may sell or distribute electronic mail addresses collected, nor may they knowingly use an electronic mail address, the collection, sale, and distribution of which are prohibited, in violation of paragraph 1.
