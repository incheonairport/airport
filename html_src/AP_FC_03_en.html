Traveling with a Vulnerable Passenger

Transportation and parking

Disability parking space

For your convenience, there are short-term disability parking areas near each airport counter.
There are disability parking area in the short-term parking lot (Level 1, Basement 1) and near the elevator and shuttle buses in the long-term parking lot.

Disability standby area

There is a specially marked standby area for people with disability on the Passenger Terminal road (directly in front of Exit 3 and 12).

Help-phone service

Pick up the receiver to be directly connected to the nearest Information Desk Hot Line.

All shuttles on the Passenger Terminal↔Long-Term Parking Lot, Cargo Terminal, and other routes are equipped with a wheelchair lift to increase the convenience for disabled, senior, or other vulnerable passengers.

Valet parking service

Parking is made easy and safe with the airport-approved valet parking service(at a fee).

Compact cars, disabled licensed car, and veterans’ cars are eligible for a discount when using this service.

Security check & Immigration process

Fast Track

  • In order to use Fast Track at the Incheon Airport, vulnerable passengers (persons registered as disabled, minors under 7 years of age, seniors over 70 years of age, pregnant mothers, 3 companions included) will need to verify their status at the airline’s check-in counter and receive the “Fast Track Pass” to use the exclusive departure area entrance.

Services within the airport

Elderly support service

  • Korean Air provides “One Family Service” for elderly passengers who are traveling abroad for the first time. The assigned airline employee will guide the passenger through the departure/arrival process, including the immigration process, inspection and claiming baggage, and getting to the Arrival Hall.
  • For elderly passengers transferring, the service guide can assist you to the transfer counter and to the departure gate. The service is free of charge for passengers traveling to or from far distances such as the Americas, Europe, and Oceania.

Disabled-accessible bathrooms

  • Each bathroom inside the Passenger Terminal has at least 1 disabled-accessible bathroom.
  • For the convenience of users, the disabled-accessible bathroom has a wide entrance, large interior space, automatic door operated by a button, alarm bell, and automatic flush toilets. We do our best to ensure that it is a safe and convenient place.

Wheelchair rental

  • You can rent wheelchairs and strollers free of charge at any information desk within the airport.

Braille brochure

  • Braille brochures are available at any information desk within the airport.

Text message Q&A service

Text your question or comment to Incheon Airport Help Desk (1577-2600) for a response.
