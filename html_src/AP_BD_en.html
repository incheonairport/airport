Information for greeters awaiting arrivals

  • The flight might arrive earlier than the estimated arrival time, so greeters should arrive at the airport with enough time to spare.
  • Airport visitors can verify the arrival time and designated exit on the large information screen in front of the Arrival Hall.
  • Be advised that if the arriving passengers leave through another exit than the designated one to get to the Floor 1 Arrival Hall, they might not be able to locate the greeters waiting for them.
