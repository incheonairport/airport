FAQ

Q. Should I pass through the immigration in order to join the transit tour?

You need to pass through the immigration to join the transit tour. Also, you have to pass through the immigration and security check after the tour.

Q. Where can I register for the tour?

You can register for the tour at the main desk (1st Floor) after the immigration.

Q. Does the tour time include travel time to the destination?

Every tour includes travel time to destination and return way. You will be back to the airport by the end of the tour time.

Q. Is there any place I can put my hand-carry baggage during the tour?

You can bring your baggage to the tour bus with you or leave your baggage at our main desk during the transit tour. (No charge, only for transit tour passengers). Please note that you need to take valuable things with you and you have to take your baggage before 18:00.
