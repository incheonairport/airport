Passport
  • Take special care of your passport as it serve a very important function of proving your identity.
  • In the case of lost or stolen passport during international travel, visit the closest embassy or consulate general to report a loss passport and receive a travel certificate or a temporary passport
  • In the case of a damaged or tampered passport, you might be prohibited from getting a boarding pass or traveling abroad. Please reissue your passport.
Regular Passport Permanent Resident Passport Official or Diplomatic Passport Travel Certificate Passport application fee
Visa
  • Contact the destination country’s embassy in Korea regarding visas.
  • Korean nationals may enter countries that have signed the “Visa Exemption Agreement” with Korea.
  • Electronic travel certificate is required to enter the U.S. (ESTA) and Canada (eTA), and to enter the U.K., you must have your photo ID, proof of employment, return flight ticket, lodging information, and travel itinerary.

International driver’s license
  • Travelers wishing to drive while abroad can visit one of the 190 sites that issue international driver’s license with passports. Visit the website for more information.

Travel safety information

Anyone found travelling to Level 4 travel-ban countries is punishable under Passport Act Article 26 and subject to up to 1 year of incarceration or paying up to 3,000,000 KRW.

International Travel Safety website

International flight airport fee/Travel fee/International Fund for Disease Eradication
Type Description Note
Applicable to All passengers traveling abroad on any flight departing from Incheon Airport  
Applicable fee
  • International passenger airport fee: departing passengers 17,000 KRW per person, layover passenger 10,000 KRW
  • Travel fee: departing passenger 10,000 KRW per person
  • International Fund for Disease Eradication: departing passenger 1,000 KRW per person
Included in your airfare
Exemption
  • Passengers with diplomatic passports
  • Children under the age of 2
  • Children being adopted abroad and their companion
  • Foreign military personnel or official residing in Korea
  • Passengers who are being sent back after being denied entry
  • Foreigners being deported by the government
  • Approved passengers who must leave the bonded area for departure due to:
    • aircraft failure and must depart the same day or the next day
    • aircraft departure being delayed by airport closure or weather related issues
    • inevitable emergency landing by aircraft failure or hijacking or medical emergency
  • Layover passengers who leave the bonded area for tourism and come back for departure within 24 hours

* In the case that the fees have already been included, passengers with a diplomatic passport (except for when GTR is issued) and layover passengers who leave the bonded area for tourism and come back for departure within 24 hours can visit the Refund Counter, Shin-Han Bank/KEB Hana Bank/Woori Bank exchange desk) for a refund. For all over exemptions, the fee is already excluded from airfare.

For a refund, present: diplomatic passport, e-ticket receipt (with Tax details), boarding pass
