Lost or Damaged Baggage

Damaged baggage

  1. ① The best thing to do is to immediately report the damaged baggage to the lost baggage information desk located behind the baggage claim area. If you show the damaged baggage to the airline employee, the airline will process the issue.
  2. ② Any damaged baggage is the responsibility of each airline. Compensation will be processed according to each airlines refund policy, but there might be difficulty in claiming compensation might be difficult.
  3. ③ Even if you have already left the airport and cannot file a claim in person, please call the airline directly to address the issue.

Check the airport website for a listing of airline contact information. Airline contact information

Lost baggage

  • Please contact your airline directly as each airline manages all the baggage and the compensation policy varies between airlines.
  • If you are departing from Incheon Airport and arriving at another airport, please contact the destination airport regarding any lost items.
