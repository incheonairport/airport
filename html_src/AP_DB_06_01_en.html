Parking Guide

  • The parking lot is divided into car, bus, taxi and long-term parking(outdoors) sections. Passengers can take the airport shuttle bus to get to the arrival level at the terminal.
  • Airport shuttle buses run between the parking lot and Arrivals (1st floor) every 16 minutes from 04:00~06:00, and 22:00~24:00 and every 8 minutes from 06:00~22:00
  • For people with disability, free lift service is available (Call 032-741-3217).
  • Valet Parking : Valet Parking is available at the eastern stops/drop off points on the 3rd floor of the terminal
