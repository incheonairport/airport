Warning

  • There might be restrictions on airline and flight routes dependeing on visa and documentation.
  • You can check your own baggage (under 90cm length) and please lay 1 each at a time on its side.
  • Use the TUB for bags with long handles or light bags.
  • Check flammables, lighters, and other prohibited items before hand, and use the in-person counter for checking in animals/plants.
  • Please contact your airline for details.
