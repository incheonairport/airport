Roaming service information

  • If you are using the roaming service for the first time, please visit the website and Roaming Center for your carrier (SKT, KT, LGU+) located inside Incheon International Airport
  • The type of roaming service can change depending on your phone and destination country, so please visit your carrier's website or customer center (SKT, KT, LGU+) before departure.
  • The wattage and electric plug might vary across country, so check your cell phone, charger, and battery before departure.
  • You must turn off the international phone call restriction service for automatic roaming service. You cannot dial numbers such as 15XX or 080, but when you are able to connect, you can be carhged for the ARS connection fee, so be aware of this.
