At the City-Airport Transit Terminal

City Airport Terminal

Passengers who complete the boarding procedure at the City-Airport Transit Terminals can use the express tunnel at Incheon Airport to proceed directly through security check and immigration process.

  • Seoul Station City-Airport Transit Terminal
  • Samsung Station City-Airport Transit Terminal
  • Gwangmyeong Station City-Airport Transit Terminal

Location

  • Samsung Station city-airport transit terminal 1st floor

Services provided

  • Boarding procedure, check baggage, immigration process (Restricted to international flights departing the same day from Incheon Airport)

Operating hours

  • Boarding procedure, check baggage : 05:20 ~ 18:30 (Finish boarding procedure 3 hours before departure)
  • Immigration process : 06:00 ~ 18:30

Using city-airport transit terminal

Assigned seating, baggage drop, and departure process at the City Airport Terminal Getting to Incheon Airport Use express tunnel straight ahead Security Check Pass through immigration desks reserved for city-airport transit customers Proceed to boarding gate Board

Using the express tunnel

  • Passengers who complete the boarding procedure as well as the immigration process at the Samsung Station City-Airport Transit Terminal may use the express tunnel to Incheon Airport Floor 3 Departure Area.

Using the City-Airport Transit Terminal

  • Not all airlines provide services at the City-Airport Transit Terminals. Please contact the appropriate terminal to find out which airlines are serviced.
