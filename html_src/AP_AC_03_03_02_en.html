Quarantine Inspection

Quarantine Inspection guidelines

Quarantine Declaration

The Quarantine Inspection Station issues inspections and certificates for foreign travelers, animals, and plants. Depending on the destination country, you might be required to submit an inspection certificate, so please check ahead of time.

You must complete the quarantine inspection related procedure before boarding procedure to avoid any delays in boarding.

  • Passenger Inspection
  • animal / plant Inspection

Animal inspection

  • Before leaving the country with a companion animal, please check with the destination country’s animal quarantine inspection agency or its embassy in Korea as countries vary in required documents (health certificate, proof of rabies and other vaccination, etc.) and which animals are allowed or banned from entry.
  • Please be sure with enquire with appropriate agencies especially if you are taking a dog or a cat to Japan, Taiwan, Australia, New Zealand, or European Union countries.

Requirements for animal inspection

  • On the day of departure, bring your companion animal and required documents (health certificate, proof of rabies vaccine, rabies test result, etc.) to request inspection at the Animal and Plant Quarantine Inspection Agency Inspection Room. You will be issued a certificate of inspection if there are no problems.
  • Required documents : Proof of rabies vaccine, health certificate, and other inspection requirements for each country
  • If you plan on returning to Korea within a short time period, please check the requirements (rabies test result, microchip, etc.) for entering Korea with a companion animal.
