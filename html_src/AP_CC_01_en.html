Transfer Tour Information

  • All tours are given in English.
  • Tour Program might change without prior notice due to inevitable situations.
  • Tour reservation is filled on a first-come-first-served basis.
  • At the time of tour reservation, you must have the transfer passenger’s passport, arrival/departure boarding pass or e-ticket.

If you are denied entry by Immigration Office, you cannot attend the Transfer Tour. Please direct any questions about entry process and visas to the Ministry of Justice.

Only transfer passengers in the Passenger Terminals may attend the Transfer Tour. (Transfer passengers in the Concourse cannot participate in the Transfer Tour.)
