Using City-Airport Transit

City Airport Terminal

Passengers who complete the boarding procedure at the City-Airport Transit Terminals can use the express tunnel at Incheon Airport to proceed directly through security check and immigration process.

  • Seoul Station City-Airport Transit Terminal
  • Samsung Station City-Airport Transit Terminal
  • Gwangmyeong Station City-Airport Transit Terminal

Location

  • KTX Gwangmyeong Station Basement 1

Services provided

  • Boarding procedure, check baggage, immigration process (Restricted to international flights departing the same day from Incheon Airport)

Boarding procedure and baggage check

You can experience a faster and more convenient boarding procedure services such as choosing your seat and checking bags at the Gwangmyeong Station City-Airport Transit Terminal desk. You can claim your checked bags at your final destination rather than at Incheon Airport.

  • Available hours : 06:30 ~ 19:00 (Finish boarding procedure 3 hours before departure)
  • Airlines operating at the station : Korean Air, Asiana, Jeju Air, T’way, Air Seoul, and Estar Airlines (see website for details)

Departure immigration procedure

You can have your immigration inspection quickly and more easily at the Ministry of Justice counter after you finish your check-in process.

  • Operating hours : 07:00 ~ 19:00
  • Necessary documentation : boarding pass, passport

Riding the limousine

Passengers can take the KTX Limousine exclusive stop from West Exit 4 of Gwangmyeong Station Floor 1 after the check-in and immigration procedure.

  • Operating hours : 06:00 ~ 22:10
  • Duration : 20min ~ 30min

Arriving passengers can use the KTX Limousine to Gwangmyeong Station to get to the KTX more quickly and more coveniently.

Using the express tunnel

  • Passengers who complete the boarding procedure as well as the immigration process at Gwangmyeong Station City-Airport Transit Terminal may use the express tunnel to Incheon Airport Floor 3 Departure Area

Steps

  • Immigration Inspection
  • Check-in Counter
  • Print boarding pass and check bags
  • Security Check
  • Loading dock
  • Airport Limousine
  • Airport curbside
  • Express entry tunnel
  • Security Check
  • Immigration Inspection
  • bus system
  • Board plane
