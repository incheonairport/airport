Baggage Claim

Baggage claim process

  1. step 01
    After completing the immigration process, check the baggage claim area number.
  2. step 02
    Take the escalators or elevators to Floor 1.
  3. step 03
    Wait by the designated baggage claim area.
  4. step 04
    Pick up your baggage.

    Please verify that the baggage is your and not someone else’s.

위탁 수하물

  • 입국심사를 마치고 전면 전광판을 통해 수하물수취대 번호를 확인한 후 에스컬레이터를 타고 1층으로 이동합니다.

Oversized baggage

  • Proceed to the oversized baggage claim area.
