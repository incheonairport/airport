Transferring from Passenger Terminal 2

Only Passengers without a boarding pass should use the Transfer Desk

  • Passenger Terminal 2 → Passenger Terminal 2
  • Passenger Terminal 2 → Concourse
  • Passenger Terminal 2 → Passenger Terminal 1
  1. Arrival
    1

    Arrive at Passenger Terminal 2 Floor 2

  2. Transfer Desk
    2

    Passengers without a boarding pass must visit the Transfer Desk at Passenger Terminal 2 Floor 2.

  3. Pass through ATG
    3

    Pass through the Auto Transfer Gate (ATG) at Passenger Terminal 2 Floor 2 for transfer.

  4. Transfer Desk
    4

    Go through Security Check for transfer like regular departure

    Transfer Security Check
  5. Departure
    5

    Proceed to Boarding Area in Passenger Terminal 2 Floor 3.
