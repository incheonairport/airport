Instructions

  • The Incheon International Airport Lost and Found is a facility to manage issues related to lost and found items in and around the airport, and it is currently managed by the 『Incheon International Airport Police Force』.
  • Found items (except for IDs, passports, and food items) are stored for the legally mandated period of 6 months and 3 months for the finder to claim ownership, then turned over to the National Treasury or be disposed of. . Food items: store for 1 week, then dispose.
  • Passport: store for 1 month, then send to issuing agency (corresponding city hall, borough office, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, embassy, etc.) (Ministry of Foreign Affairs 02-3210-0404)
