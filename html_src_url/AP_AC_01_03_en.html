Web/Mobile Check-in

You can easily check-in for your flight through your airline’s web/mobile homepage.

Baggage

  • If you have baggage to be checked, you must visit the airline’s assigned counter to drop it off.

Warning

  • Some airlines do not offer web/mobile check-in service.
  • Please check with each airline to see they offer this service.
