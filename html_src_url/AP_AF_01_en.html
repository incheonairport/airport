DOMESTIC FLIGHT
01
Arrive at the Terminal

You can check-in for domestic flights on Floor 1 of the Terminal.

02
Boarding Procedure
Check Baggage

Go through the boarding procedure at the transfer flight airline counter.

Domestic flight counters : Floor 1, near Exit 3

03
Proceed to
Security Check

After check-in proceed to the departure tunnel used exclusively for domestic flights.

04
Security
Check

Present your photo ID and boarding pass to the security agent for security check.

Photo ID: residence card, drivers license, passport, etc.

05
Proceed to
Boarding Gate

Proceed to gate (1-50) as indicated on your boarding pass.

Board the plane at least 15 minutes before departure time.
