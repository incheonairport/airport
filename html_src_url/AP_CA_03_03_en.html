Transferring from Passenger Terminal 2

Only Passengers without a boarding pass should use the Transfer Desk

  • Passenger Terminal 2 → Passenger Terminal 1
  • Passenger Terminal 2 → Concourse
  • Passenger Terminal 2 → Passenger Terminal 2
  1. Arrival
    1

    Arrive at Passenger Terminal 2 Floor 2

  2. Pass through ATG
    2

    Pass through the Auto Transfer Gate (ATG) at Passenger Terminal 2 Floor 2 for transfer.

  3. Proceed to Concourse
    3

    Use the Shuttle Train from Passenger Terminal 2 Basement 1 to get to the Concourse.

  4. Pass through ATG
    4

    Pass through the Auto Transfer Gate (ATG) at Concourse Basement 1 for transfer.

  1. Proceed to Passenger Terminal 1
    5

    Use the Shuttle Train from Concourse Basement 1 to get to Passenger Terminal 1.

  2. Transfer Security Check
    6

    Go through Security Check for transfer like regular departure.

    Transfer Security Check
  3. Transfer Desk
    7

    Passengers without a boarding pass must visit the Transfer Desk at Passenger Terminal 1 Floor 4.

  4. Departure
    8

    Proceed to Boarding Area in Passenger Terminal 1 Floor 3.
