Transferring from Passenger Terminal 1

Only Passengers without a boarding pass should use the Transfer Desk

  • Passenger Terminal 1 → Passenger Terminal 1
  • Passenger Terminal 1 → Concourse
  • Passenger Terminal 1 → Passenger Terminal 2
  1. Arrival
    1

    Arrive at Passenger Terminal 1 Floor 2.

  2. Transfer Security Check
    2

    Go through Security Check for transfer like regular departure.

    Transfer Security Check
  3. Transfer Desk
    3

    Passengers without a boarding pass must visit the Transfer Desk at Passenger Terminal 1 Floor 4.

  4. Departure
    4

    Proceed to Boarding Area in Passenger Terminal 1 Floor 3.
