Regular Security Check

  • After completing boarding procedure and customs declaration, proceed to the nearest departure area for security check.
  • If the nearest departure area is crowded, use another departure area.
  • We conduct security checks for the safety of passengers and the operating aircraft, so please comply with the security check.
  • For safe operation of flights, visitors who are not traveling cannot enter beyond the security check area, so please complete farewells before proceeding to the departure area.

Security check procedure

  1. step 01
    Present your passport and boarding pass to the security agent.
  2. step 02
    If you have items to be reported/declared, visit the Customs desk before security check.
  3. step 03

    Place all items on the x-ray conveyor belt (backpack, handbag, tote, etc.)

    Depending on the destination, there might be additional security checks of shoes, etc.

  4. step 04
    Place personal belongings (phone, wallet, key, coins, etc.) in a basket.
  5. step 05

    Passenger Terminal 1 Security Check: walk through the detectors with gates and be inspected by a security agent.

    Passenger Terminal 2 Security Check walk through the detectors with gates and be inspected by a security agent.

    There might be additional inspection depending on the destination.

    Do not fill out the departure reporting form.
